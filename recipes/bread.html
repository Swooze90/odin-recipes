Delicious Soda Bread

Description

Have you ever wanted to try irish soda bread?
Don't even have a clue what it is?
Don't worry, we got you covered with this simple and easy recipe to get you started!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 3 TBS sugar
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup cold unsalted butter cut into small cubes
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1/3 cup raisins or currents

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees
  2. Whisk together the flour, sugar, baking soda, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl.
  3. Cut the cold butter into 1/4 inch cubes and add it to the flour mixture.
  4. Using a pastry cutter or your hands work the butter into the flour until it resembles course
