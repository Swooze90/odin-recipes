Famous Meatloaf

Description

This meatloaf is a wonderful and easy to make recipe that was made originally by my wife.
While I was never a huge meatloaf fan to begin with, hers is where I found my real appreciation for the loaf of meat.
All of the ingredients listed can be found at your local store.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon good olive oil
  • 3 cups chopped yellow onions (3 onions)
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh thyme leaves
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/3 cup canned chicken stock or broth
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 2.5 pounds ground chuck (81 percent lean)
  • 1/2 cup plain dry bread crumbs (Progresso)
  • 2 extra-large eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup ketchup (Heinz)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325
  2. Throw all ingredients in large mixing bowl
  3. Mix by hand until satisfied
  4. Take out baking sheet, cover with foil
  5. Place meatloaf on sheet
  6. Leave in oven for 25 minutes depending on internal temperature
